Find Answers To Freuqently Asked Questions

Q: What printer should I use?
A: We have found the best printer to buy for quality and price, is the Ender 3 Pro 3D printer. It is typically around $200-$300 USD and can be bought from amazon if you wish to start printing your own designs at home.
Q: What material should I use?
A: We have found the best material for printing to be from companies like Hatchbox on amazon. You can buy cheap spools to use and they are very reliable.
Q: Where can I find printing patterns?
A: There are many online websites that will offer free patterns made and uploaded by users. You can find many patterns on Thingiverse.com. You can also use TinkerCad app, a browser based app great for those new to printing.
Q: What are supports?
A: Supports are typically caluclated by your own software and will help to create little stands that woill support parts that are sticking out while printing. You can remove the supports from the prodcut once they are finished.
